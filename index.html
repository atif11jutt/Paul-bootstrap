The Blouin Family has been selling and servicing Performance Vehicles in Central Maine since 1925. We are a family owned business that has built a solid reputation by providing unmatched value for our customers.

We invite you to visit our 24,000 square foot Superstore located at 513 Western Ave, Augusta, Maine. Paul Blouin Performance is the largest Powersports /Automotive Dealership in all of New England where you'll find over 450 new and used vehicles in stock. We also have the largest selection of new Mazda vehicles, new Victory-Motorcycles®, new Triumph motorcycles, and new Suzuki motorcycles in Maine.

It's our goal to stock every model and color option for all of the brands we carry.
